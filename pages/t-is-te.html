For this project, some of my tasks included:

  • Developing the four-episode web series for The Black Collective Media with a $5,000 budget
  • Creating and managing the shooting schedule and logistics for a cast of 13 and a crew of 4
  • Guiding the cast, hosts, and crew on shot requirements during the shoot
  • Filling in as assembly editor using Adobe Premiere Pro
  • Overseeing post-production of each episode, which aired weekly in February 2022
'T is for Té is a four-episode web series that launched in February 2022 to celebrate Black History Month.

The series highlights notable members of PEI's Black community, showcasing their stories and contributions.

Produced in Charlottetown, Prince Edward Island, 'T is for Té was created under the Black Collective Media banner
